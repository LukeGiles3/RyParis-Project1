HAMBURGER OF THE WEEK
Time
Level: Easy Prep: 10 min Yield: 4 hamburgers
Total: 25 min Cook: 15 min

BENGAL BURGER

★★★★★ 26 reviews

📠

No more dry lackluster burgers. These are juicy and spices can be easily added or changed to suit anyone's tastes. Baste it frequently with your favorite barbeque sauce. If you find the meat mixture to be too mushy, just add more bread crumbs until it forms patties that hold their shape.

    Ingredients
  • 24 ounces groung chuck
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • Freshly groung black pepper
  • Spice mix, optional, recipe follows
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 4 slices cheese, such as Cheddar, American
  • 4 soft hamburger style buns, split
  • 4 slices beefsteak tomatoes, optional
  • Assorted lettuces and greens, such as iceberg
  • Assorted mustards, optional
  • Mayonaise, optional
  • Ketchup, optional
